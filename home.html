#codingEdge

#codingEdge brings the exciting world of computer programming to high school students. This new camp, run by ambitious UBC students, aims to introduce the concepts programming to students in grades 8 to 12 using Java.

Why #codingEdge?
Learn from experienced UBC students who know what it takes to enter the computer industry.
Create cool applications
Gain the knowledge required to make the computer do what you want.
Enhance your resume
The technology industry is always on the lookout for individuals with excellent computing background.
Final Project - Connect Four

By the end of the course, students will be able to create a command line Connect Four game based on concepts they learn in class. This simple program will teach the basics of software development, algorithms, and design patterns.
